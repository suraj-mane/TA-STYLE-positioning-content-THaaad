Apple iPhone SE smartphone was launched in March 2016. The phone comes with a 4.00-inch touchscreen display with a resolution of 640x1136 pixels at a pixel density of 326 pixels per inch (ppi). Apple iPhone SE is powered by an one-core Apple A9 processor. It comes with 2GB of RAM.
